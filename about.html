Vision

We envision Pasong Tamo as transformed Barangay with highly empowered residents living in a peaceful and orderly residential community and with business friendly environment where shared responsibility in local governance reigns towards a Quality Community within a Quality City

Mission

To enhance poverty reduction, preservation of environment, equitable economic opportunities, protection of individual rights, need driven, justifiable and sustainable support from the government, private sector participation in local governance through adoptive guidance, representations and empowerment measures from the local government.

Goals:

  • Poverty Alleviation.
  • Conservation and protection of environment.
  • People empowerment resources base.
  • Promotion of equal and fair economic opportunities.
  • Development of strong economic base capable of generating self-sustaining growth.
  • Equality and social justice.
  • Upgrading of capability of Barangay Officials and Personnel.
  • Effective and Efficient delivery of government services.
  • Promotion of Effective Participation and cooperation between & among government, non-government and people organization.
